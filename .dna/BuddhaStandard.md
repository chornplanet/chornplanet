# 1. Purpose

To define a purified, production-grade Buddha visual standard under ChornDNA that emphasizes serenity, balance, sacred
geometry, and timeless elegance — while removing decorative excess and cosmic background architecture.

This standard is intended for high-fidelity sacred rendering, design systems, sculpture modeling, digital generation,
and cinematic visualization.

---

# 2. Core Visual Identity

## 2.1 Spiritual Essence

* Absolute calm
* Inner stillness
* Compassionate neutrality
* Enlightened presence
* Silent strength

Expression must be:

* Eyes gently lowered (meditative gaze)
* Soft, symmetrical smile (micro-expression only)
* Relaxed facial muscles
* No dramatic emotion

## 3.1 Body Structure

* Idealized balanced proportions
* Harmonious shoulder width
