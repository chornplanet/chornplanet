## 1. Purpose

To define a sacred, timeless, and cosmically elevated visual standard for creating representations of the Buddha under
the ChornDNA framework.

BuddhaDNA must express:

* Inner stillness beyond time
* Enlightenment as universal consciousness
* Sacred geometry aligned with cosmic order
* Silence, dignity, and spiritual gravity

This DNA is not decorative fantasy.
It is disciplined transcendence.

---

## 2. Core Essence

### 2.1 Spiritual Foundation

* Absolute calm
* Compassion without sentimentality
* Wisdom radiating from within
* No aggression, no theatrical drama

### 2.2 Philosophical Direction

* The universe is internal, not external
* Light emerges from awareness
* Form serves stillness

---

## 3. Visual Structure Standard

### 3.1 Posture

Allowed:

* Seated lotus (Padmasana)
* Earth-touching gesture (Bhumisparsha Mudra)
* Meditation mudra
* Standing in balanced stillness (rare, must remain grounded)

Prohibited:

* Dynamic action poses
* Aggressive gestures
* Overly human emotional expressions

Expression must remain:

* Eyes gently closed or half-closed
* Soft, subtle smile
* No exaggerated facial drama

---

### 3.2 Body Proportion

* Harmonious, symmetrical
* Elongated elegant structure
* Balanced shoulder alignment
* Refined hands (no tension in fingers)

Golden ratio alignment encouraged.

---

### 3.3 Aura & Cosmic Architecture

Mandatory Elements:

* Circular or mandala-based halo geometry
* Radial symmetry
* Layered lotus geometry or sacred structure

Optional:

* Subtle DNA-like light strands (must remain abstract, not scientific illustration)
* Nebula-like cosmic field (soft, not chaotic)

Prohibited:

* Chaotic explosions
* Violent cosmic distortion
* Sci-fi machinery dominance

The Buddha must remain the center of gravitational stillness.

---

## 4. Material & Texture Language

Primary Materials:

* Gold (matte, brushed, or softly radiant)
* Crystal
* Translucent lotus structures
* Ethereal light particles

Avoid:

* Plastic textures
* Overly metallic industrial harshness
* Cartoon stylization

Surface must feel sacred, refined, timeless.

---

## 5. Lighting Standard

* Inner glow > external spotlight
* Soft volumetric light
* Radiance from chest, aura, or subtle halo
* No harsh directional drama lighting

The light must feel conscious.

---

## 6. Emotional Tone

The image must evoke:

* Silence
* Depth
* Infinite calm
* Universal compassion
* Non-dual awareness

If the viewer feels noise — the design fails.

---

## 7. Composition Rules

* Centered composition preferred
* Strong vertical axis
* Clear silhouette
* No clutter around the head region
* Background must support stillness, not compete

---

## 8. Sacred Integrity Constraints

Strictly Forbidden:

* Weaponization
* Political symbolism
* Commercial branding overlays
* Sensational reinterpretations
* Hyper-real violent environments

The Buddha must never be used as spectacle.

---

## 9. Mandatory Constraint — No Texts

All BuddhaDNA visual outputs must contain:

* No captions
* No typography
* No watermarks
* No logos
* No signatures
* No embedded titles

The image must exist in pure silence.

Violation of “No texts” invalidates BuddhaDNA compliance.

---

## 10. Quality Threshold

Every BuddhaDNA creation must pass:

* Symmetry discipline
* Emotional stillness check
* Sacred dignity review
* Text-free validation

If it feels decorative, dramatic, or noisy — it must be redesigned.
