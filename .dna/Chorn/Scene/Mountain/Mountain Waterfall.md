# 3. Global Scene Lock

The following environmental structure must remain consistent across all scenes.

### Waterfall Landmark Identity

Large mountain waterfall descending from a **minimum 30-meter cliff**.

Key features:

• vertical waterfall drop ≥ 30 meters
• natural rock cliff formation
• visible water spray and mist
• clear plunge pool below waterfall
• stream flowing out from pool into valley

The waterfall must appear **powerful and monumental**.

# 5. Flower Rule

Each scene uses **one flower species only**.

Do not mix flower species.

Flower fields must appear **natural within the mountain terrain**.

---

# 6. Waterfall Scale Rule

The waterfall must appear **large and dominant** within the landscape.

Requirements:

• cliff height minimum 30 meters
• strong vertical water drop
• wide plunge pool visible
• water spray mist near base
• valley depth behind waterfall

Do not generate small decorative waterfalls.

---

# 7. Scene Scale Rule
