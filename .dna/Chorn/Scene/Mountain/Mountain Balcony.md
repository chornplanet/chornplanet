# 4. Cinematic Engine (NEW)

Mountain Balcony v3 introduces a **Global Cinematic Rendering Engine**.

Every generated scene must include:

### Panoramic Landscape Scale

The environment must feel vast.

Requirements:

• wide valley openness
• visible horizon line
• layered mountain depth
• panoramic landscape perspective

The viewer must feel:

**standing above a mountain valley from a balcony viewpoint.**

---

### 4-Layer Cinematic Depth System

Each scene must maintain **four visual layers**.

Layer 1 — Balcony Foreground
• balcony architecture
• railing
• furniture

Layer 2 — Near-Field Environment
• flowers
• character interaction objects

Layer 3 — Mid-Ground Landscape
• mountain stream
• valley vegetation

Layer 4 — Background Landscape
• distant mountains
• soft atmospheric mist

This structure prevents **background collapse or blur loss**.

---

### Near-Field Detail Preservation

Objects near the character must remain sharp.

Includes:

• flowers
• books
• tea cups
• tables
• vases

Petal texture and object detail must be visible.

---

### Stream Physics Rendering

The mountain stream must remain visible and detailed.

Required:

• water texture
• reflective sunlight
• small ripples
• visible stones in shallow areas

The stream must never become:

• flat texture
• blurred background
