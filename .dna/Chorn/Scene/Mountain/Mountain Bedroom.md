# 3. Global Scene Lock

The following architectural and environmental rules must remain consistent across all scenes.

### Architecture Identity

Romantic luxury mountain bedroom with **floor-to-ceiling glass wall**.

Key elements:

• full panoramic glass panel
• king-size bed positioned beside glass
• natural luxury materials
• minimal refined furniture

Interior must frame nature, not compete with it.

---

### Landscape Structure

Exterior landscape must include:

Foreground:

flower field

Mid-ground:

open valley landscape

Background layer 1:

mountain ridges

Background layer 2:

soft floating mountain mist

Background layer 3:

clear open sky

---

### Flower Rule

Each scene must contain **one flower species only**.

Do not mix flower types.

Flowers must appear natural within the valley landscape.

---

### Lighting Rule

Clear Day only.

Lighting must be:

• soft natural daylight
• neutral white tone
• balanced interior and exterior exposure

Not allowed:

• sunset lighting
• golden hour tones
• artificial warm interior lighting
