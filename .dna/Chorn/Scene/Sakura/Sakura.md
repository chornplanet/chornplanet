# Identity

Luxury **living corner viewpoint** overlooking a majestic **Living Sakura Landscape**.

The scene represents a **realistic human living corner integrated with blooming sakura nature**, such as a sakura garden
terrace, wooden viewing deck, garden corner, or stone platform.

Foreground living corner → sakura garden or petal field → sakura valley or sakura grove → distant mountain horizon.

The sakura biome represents **seasonal spring landscapes**, where sakura trees bloom across valleys, gardens,
riversides, and hills.

The purpose of this scene system is to create **cinematic luxury spring campaign visuals where human life peacefully
coexists with the beauty of a blooming sakura environment**.
