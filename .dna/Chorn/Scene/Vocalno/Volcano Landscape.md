# Identity

Luxury **living corner viewpoint** overlooking a majestic **Living Volcano Landscape**.

The scene represents a **realistic human living corner integrated with volcanic nature**, such as a volcanic lodge
terrace, stone viewing platform, geothermal garden deck, or crater viewing corner.

Foreground living corner → volcanic terrain → lava valley or geothermal formations → volcano mountain horizon.

The volcano biome represents **powerful geological landscapes**, featuring lava valleys, volcanic ridges, geothermal
fields, and dramatic crater mountains.

The volcano is **active but stable**, producing warm lava glow and geothermal energy without violent eruptions.

The purpose of this scene system is to create **cinematic luxury volcanic campaign visuals where human life peacefully
coexists with the majestic power of volcanic landscapes**.

```

```

Examples

The invocation command references the **scene label** rather than numbers, allowing **AI Scene Engine** to interpret
volcanic environments accurately.
