# Identity

Luxury **living corner viewpoint** overlooking a majestic **Golden Rice Valley Landscape**.

The scene represents a **realistic human living corner integrated with fertile agricultural valley environments**, such
as a rice terrace balcony, wooden farmhouse deck, bamboo garden platform, or valley lodge terrace.

Foreground living corner → golden rice terraces → river valley → distant mountain horizon.

The Golden Rice Valley biome represents **fertile agricultural landscapes**, where vast rice terraces and golden rice
fields create a harmonious relationship between human civilization and natural landforms.

The purpose of this scene system is to create **cinematic luxury agricultural valley campaign visuals where human life
peacefully coexists with golden rice landscapes**.

---

# Invocation Command

```
GoldenRice: {scene label}
```

Examples

```
GoldenRice: Golden Terrace Balcony
GoldenRice: Rice Valley Panorama Deck
GoldenRice: Bamboo Farm Viewing Corner
```

The invocation command references the **scene label** rather than numbers, enabling **AI Scene Engine** to interpret
agricultural valley environments correctly.
