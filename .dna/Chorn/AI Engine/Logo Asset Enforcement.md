# Logo Asset Enforcement (Critical)

### Logo source

```
Use ONLY the logo from the attached file.
Do NOT generate or redraw the logo.
Do NOT create a new constellation symbol.
```

### Rendering method

The logo appears as a **digital display screen embedded in the train body**, showing the provided logo image.

---

### Attachment rule

The logo must be:

* flush mounted to the train body
* permanently attached to the side panel
* coplanar with the train surface
* not floating
* not detached

---

### Rotation rule

The **logo image rotates slowly as a circular display on its own surface plane**.

Motion:

```
slow clockwise rotation
2D rotation on the panel surface
rotation center fixed
```

---

### Absolute prohibitions

```
do not create a new logo
do not redesign the logo
do not generate constellation graphics
do not float the logo
do not detach the logo
do not slide the logo
do not tilt the logo in 3D
do not spin the logo fast
```
