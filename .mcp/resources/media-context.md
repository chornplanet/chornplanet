# Media Context

ChornPlanet can become a daily media platform for news, entertainment, technology, and civilization-commerce content.

## Media categories

Initial daily media categories:

- news follow-up
- entertainment follow-up
- technology follow-up
- outfit / clothing / civilization posts
- premium commerce posts
- Smart Food stories
- luxury project stories

## Daily AI media pipeline

Future direction:

```text
AI search daily content
  ↓
source review and safety check
  ↓
digest / summary
  ↓
ChornPlanet editorial angle
  ↓
image/video prompt generation
  ↓
human or approved automation review
  ↓
posting
  ↓
analytics review
```

## Editorial rules

- Use reliable sources.
- Do not copy full articles.
- Summarize and transform into ChornPlanet editorial style.
- Add citations or source tracking where needed.
- Separate factual reporting from opinion or creative interpretation.
- Do not publish legal, medical, financial, or political claims without careful review.
- Automated posting requires explicit approval before live external publishing.

## AI image/video direction

For outfit and civilization content, use DNA authority.

For news/entertainment/technology, use brand-safe visual generation and avoid misleading images.

## Growth objective

Daily media automation should support:

- more consistent posting
- SEO and LLM visibility
- repeat traffic
- TikTok/social growth
- commerce conversion
- investor-ready evidence of content engine capability
