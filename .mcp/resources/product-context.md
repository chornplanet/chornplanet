# Product Context

ChornPlanet is a long-term platform that combines media, entertainment, technology follow-up, premium commerce, Smart Food evolution, luxury projects, and ChornPlanet civilization storytelling.

## Core platform identity

ChornPlanet should grow as:

1. A news / entertainment / technology follow-up media platform.
2. A premium commerce platform.
3. A grocery and online supermarket platform.
4. A lady/gentleman outfit and civilization clothing platform.
5. A ChornPlanet civilization and world-presentation platform.
6. A Smart Food web evolution layer.
7. A luxury project showcase and product-storytelling platform.
8. An analytics-assisted growth platform.

## Growth goal

Traffic target:

```text
10K daily traffic -> 100K -> 500K -> 1M
```

Product work should be evaluated by whether it improves:

- content quality
- publishing consistency
- SEO and LLM visibility
- social shareability
- product conversion
- repeat visits
- commerce readiness
- Smart Food user retention
- analytics insight
- investor narrative

## Starting commercial wedge

The recommended starting wedge is outfit and civilization clothing posting.

Reason:

- it aligns with DNA
- it is visually attractive
- it can support social commerce
- it can link to TikTok first
- it can later expand to Shopee, Lazada, Amazon, and owned commerce
- it strengthens the civilization/luxury brand identity

## Future commerce expansion

After outfit/clothing content becomes consistent, expand toward:

- premium lifestyle products
- grocery
- online supermarket
- Smart Food order integration
- favorite meal and user preference experiences
- bookable or personalized food/lifestyle services

## Agent principle

Agents should not only generate code.

They should help ChornPlanet grow as a platform by connecting:

```text
content -> traffic -> trust -> commerce -> repeat users -> analytics -> investment readiness
```
