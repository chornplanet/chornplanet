# Commerce Context

ChornPlanet can evolve into a premium commerce platform.

## Starting commerce wedge

Start with:

- lady outfit
- gentleman outfit
- civilization clothing
- premium fashion storytelling
- DNA generated outfit/civilization posts
- TikTok-first social-commerce linking

## Future commerce categories

Expand toward:

- premium lifestyle products
- grocery
- online supermarket
- Smart Food menu/order experiences
- luxury project products and services
- books or content products
- personalized recommendations

## Marketplace direction

Priority order:

1. TikTok
2. Shopee
3. Lazada
4. Amazon
5. owned commerce / direct checkout when ready

## Product-linking principle

Content should be designed to connect naturally to commerce.

```text
Story / outfit / civilization scene
  ↓
product identity
  ↓
product page or marketplace link
  ↓
conversion tracking
  ↓
analytics insight
```

## Safety and approval

Agents may draft product content, product descriptions, and campaign plans.

Agents must not publish products, change prices, or post to external marketplaces without explicit approval.

## Investor-readiness angle

Commerce work should create evidence for:

- audience growth
- product-market fit
- conversion potential
- repeat traffic
- cross-platform commerce capability
- Smart Food integration potential
