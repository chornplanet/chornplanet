
system**, rather than a disconnected set of scenes or isolated infrastructure documents.


WorldArchitecture provides the **structural layer above the world map and mobility system**.



# I. Spatial Governance Rule

All world assets must map through the following spatial path:

```text
Scene
↓
Zone
↓
Region
↓
Continent
↓
WorldMap
```

All mobility assets must map through the following path:

```text
Station
↓
Zone
↓
Region
↓
Continent
↓
WorldMap
```

This rule ensures that:

```text
every scene belongs to a zone
every station belongs to a zone
every zone belongs to a region
every region belongs to a continent
```

This rule is mandatory for future world expansion.
