Backbone lines connect **major regions of the planet**.

They form the **primary mobility spine of ChornPlanet**.

---

```
Mountain
→ Sakura
→ Snow
→ Crystal Lake
```

```
mountain tourism
seasonal landscapes
nature exploration travel

```
