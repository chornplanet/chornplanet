# Future Tourism

## Tourism in Future Civilization

Chorn Planet explores tourism experiences integrated with intelligent infrastructure, mobility systems, luxury environments, and AI-assisted experiences.

---

## Tourism Principles

- Tourism should feel immersive
- Mobility should connect experiences seamlessly
- Environments should inspire exploration
- Smart systems should improve accessibility and convenience
- Tourism should integrate naturally with future civilization infrastructure

---

## Tourism Systems

- Coastal transportation
- Mountain mobility systems
- Smart destination connectivity
- Intelligent travel routing
- AI-assisted experiences
- Interconnected tourism districts
