# Moon And Mars Expansion

## Interplanetary Civilization Exploration

Chorn Planet explores how future civilization may evolve beyond Earth.

This includes conceptual exploration of:
- lunar infrastructure
- Mars settlements
- interplanetary mobility
- future habitat systems
- intelligent planetary environments
