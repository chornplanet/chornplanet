# AI Human Coexistence

## AI and Humanity

Chorn Planet explores futures where AI and humanity evolve together collaboratively.

AI should support:
- intelligent environments
- future infrastructure
- mobility systems
- human creativity
- smart experiences
- civilization-scale optimization

---

## Coexistence Principles

- AI should augment humanity
- AI should improve human experiences
- Intelligent systems should remain human-centered
- Technology should feel emotionally warm and accessible
- AI ecosystems should integrate naturally into daily life
