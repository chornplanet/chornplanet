# World Building Principles

## Chorn Planet Should Feel

- alive
- connected
- intelligent
- emotionally aspirational
- futuristic
- elegant
- immersive
- human-centered
- nature-integrated

---

## Civilization Atmosphere

Chorn Planet should feel like a believable future civilization ecosystem where infrastructure, mobility, tourism, architecture, AI systems, and human experiences coexist harmoniously.

---

## Avoid

- dystopian aesthetics
- overly industrial environments
- cold robotic atmospheres
- chaotic infrastructure
- disconnected systems
- visually inconsistent environments

---

## Emotional Direction

The future should feel:
- beautiful
- inspiring
- optimistic
- intelligent
- connected
