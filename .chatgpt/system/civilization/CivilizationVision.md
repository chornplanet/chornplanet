# Chorn Planet Civilization Vision

## Core Identity

Chorn Planet is an evolving future civilization platform exploring how humanity, AI, mobility, infrastructure, luxury living, digital creativity, and intelligent environments may shape the next era of living.

---

## Vision

To explore and prototype beautiful human-centered future civilizations where intelligent systems, nature, mobility, and human experience coexist harmoniously.

---

## Core Themes

- AI-integrated living
- Intelligent mobility
- Human-centered technology
- Future infrastructure
- Smart environments
- Luxury future lifestyle
- Civilization-scale systems
- Future tourism
- Interplanetary expansion
- Moon and Mars civilization concepts

---

## Platform Direction

Chorn Planet is not only:
- transportation
- AI images
- luxury content
- smart infrastructure

These are subsystems of a larger civilization vision.

The platform explores how future societies, environments, technologies, and human experiences may evolve together.
