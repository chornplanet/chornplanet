# Civilization Identity

## What Is Chorn Planet?

Chorn Planet is an evolving future civilization experience platform.

It explores how humanity, AI, intelligent infrastructure, mobility systems, luxury environments, and digital creativity may evolve together in the future.

---

## Identity Keywords

- Future civilization
- Intelligent mobility
- Smart infrastructure
- AI-integrated living
- Human-centered technology
- Luxury future lifestyle
- Intelligent tourism
- Connected environments
- Future world design
- Interplanetary civilization

## Civilization Layering

### Infrastructure Layer
Mobility systems, transportation, smart environments, intelligent logistics.

### Lifestyle Layer
Luxury living, food, tourism, fashion, and emotional experiences.

### Intelligence Layer
AI-native systems, adaptive ecosystems, intelligent interactions.

### Expansion Layer
Moon colonies, Mars civilization concepts, future planetary infrastructure.
