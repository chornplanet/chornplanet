# Visual Language

## Visual Identity

Chorn Planet visual systems should feel:
- cinematic
- futuristic
- elegant
- emotionally warm
- technologically intelligent
- globally aspirational

---

## MTS Design Principles

- Elegant futuristic architecture
- Smooth mobility systems
- Cinematic environmental lighting
- Intelligent integrated infrastructure
- Luxury minimalism
- Nature-integrated transportation
- Human-centered environments

---

## Environment Direction

### Coastal Regions
- oceanic blue tones
- tropical lighting
- smart luxury tourism
- open panoramic environments

### Mountain Regions
- mist atmosphere
- alpine environments
- elegant natural integration
- calm intelligent mobility

---

## Avoid

- harsh industrial visuals
- overcrowded interfaces
- dystopian cyberpunk overload
- visually chaotic environments
