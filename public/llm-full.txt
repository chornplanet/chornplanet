## Overview

ChornPlanet is a public Next.js platform for future civilization storytelling, smart city and smart mobility concepts, AI-native Smart Food experiences, premium style and outfit presentation, technology services, and luxury platform direction.

The site combines platform storytelling with practical public pages for AI companion work, technology capability, Smart City, Smart Mobility, Smart Food, Style, Story, About, Contact, and public policy pages.

## Public-Safety Boundary

This file is public. It does not include secrets, credentials, private customer data, private analytics configuration, unpublished marketplace operations, or internal implementation details.

If a page describes a future integration, commerce channel, automation workflow, or analytics-assisted growth direction, interpret it as public product direction unless the page explicitly states that the capability is live.
